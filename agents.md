## What GKP does

GKP acts as a discreet field partner for investors and principals who need trusted access to private markets. We:

- Source and facilitate access to **off-market real estate** in Switzerland (Zurich, Geneva, Zug, Lucerne, alpine markets)
- Provide **capital introduction**, connecting founders and asset owners with qualified private investors and family offices
- Facilitate **strategic introductions** between UHNWIs, family offices, and strategic partners
- Facilitate **business acquisitions** and succession situations in the Swiss and DACH market
- Source **hospitality and lifestyle investment** opportunities in Switzerland, Europe, and Southeast Asia

## Our positioning

GKP's value lies in access, filtering, trust, timing, and discretion, not volume. We work with a limited number of mandates at any time. We do not operate platforms, directories, or databases. Every introduction is made through genuine, trusted relationships.
