Off-Market Real Estate & Business Acquisitions

Many of the most attractive opportunities never reach public markets. Across real estate, business acquisitions, succession situations, hospitality assets, and selected private investments, transactions are often conducted discreetly through established networks, referrals, and long-standing industry relationships.

Off-Market Real Estate

Global Key Partners sources and facilitates access to off-market real estate opportunities across Switzerland and selected international markets. Through relationships with property owners, developers, investors, family offices, and industry professionals, opportunities can often be identified before reaching broader market channels.

For Buyers

Investment criteria, acquisition objectives, financial profile, and geographic preferences are established at the outset of each mandate. Opportunities are then sourced and presented selectively based on suitability, ownership verification, and transaction readiness.

For Sellers and Owners

Property owners considering a sale, refinancing, recapitalisation, or the introduction of additional capital can gain access to qualified investors, family offices, and private buyers seeking opportunities in the market. Engagements are conducted with discretion and confidentiality throughout the process.

Areas of Focus

  • Luxury residential properties, including apartments, villas, and chalets
  • Commercial and investment properties
  • Boutique hotels, resort properties, and hospitality assets
  • Development sites and land with planning potential
  • Income-producing investment properties
  • Selected international real estate opportunities

Why Off-Market Matters

Many of the most desirable assets change hands privately rather than through public listings. For buyers, this can provide access to opportunities before they become widely known. For sellers, it enables a more targeted process focused on qualified counterparties and transaction certainty.

Off-Market M&A & Business Succession

Switzerland and the wider DACH region are experiencing a significant wave of business succession, creating opportunities for investors, entrepreneurs, and strategic buyers seeking established businesses with strong foundations and long-term potential.

Global Key Partners works with business owners exploring succession or ownership transitions, as well as buyers seeking acquisition opportunities outside a public sale process.

For Business Buyers

Relationships with business owners, succession advisors, fiduciaries, and professional advisors can provide visibility into opportunities before they reach the broader market. Once acquisition criteria have been established, relevant businesses are identified and approached confidentially.

For Business Owners

Owners exploring a succession solution, partial sale, strategic partnership, management buy-in, management buy-out, or full exit may gain access to qualified buyers, investors, and acquisition partners aligned with their objectives.

Areas of Focus

  • Swiss and DACH owner-operated SMEs
  • Succession and ownership transition situations
  • Hospitality, luxury, services, and niche manufacturing businesses
  • Cross-border acquisition opportunities
  • Strategic acquisitions and growth transactions

Why Succession Matters

Many successful businesses are approaching a generational transition without a natural successor. These situations create opportunities for investors and entrepreneurs seeking established companies, experienced teams, and long-term growth potential.

How Transaction Mandates Work

Each engagement begins with a clear understanding of the opportunity, objectives, transaction criteria, and desired outcome. Based on these parameters, suitable opportunities, buyers, sellers, or investors are identified and approached selectively where appropriate.

The process is conducted with discretion, confidentiality, and a focus on achieving the right outcome for all parties involved.

Regulatory Positioning

Global Key Partners acts as an introducer and facilitator of private market opportunities.

All commercial discussions, negotiations, due diligence, documentation, and transaction decisions remain the responsibility of the parties involved and their professional advisors.

Global Key Partners operates in accordance with applicable Swiss regulations and does not provide regulated investment, legal, tax, or financial advisory services. Any such advice is provided solely by appropriately qualified professional advisors engaged by the parties.
