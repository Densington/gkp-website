2. Data We Process

Contact form

If you contact us through the contact form, we process the information you provide: name, email address, and, if you choose to share them, company, phone number, investor type, area of interest, preferred language, and the content of your message. This data is processed on the basis of your request (pre-contractual measures) and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries. Form submissions are transmitted and delivered to us via Formspree, Inc. (USA), which acts as a processor. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Email

If you email us directly, we process your email address and the content of your message in order to handle your enquiry.

Web analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google Ireland Limited (Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland) and Google LLC (USA). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to analyse how visitors use the site (pages visited, approximate location at city level, device and browser information, traffic source). IP addresses are not logged or stored by Google Analytics 4. The information generated is generally transmitted to and stored on Google servers, which may include servers in the United States. Data transfers to the USA are based on the Swiss–U.S. and EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework and/or standard contractual clauses. You can prevent analytics tracking by using the browser add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout or by disabling cookies in your browser.

Hosting and server logs

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service of GitHub, Inc. (USA). When you visit the site, GitHub automatically processes technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages requested, in server log files, for the purpose of delivering and securing the website. GitHub's privacy statement is available at docs.github.com.

3. Purposes and Legal Bases

We process personal data to operate this website, to respond to enquiries, to initiate and manage business relationships, to analyse and improve our website, and to comply with legal obligations. Where the GDPR applies, processing is based on Art. 6(1)(b) (pre-contractual measures), Art. 6(1)(f) (legitimate interests), and, where applicable, Art. 6(1)(a) (consent).

4. Disclosure and Transfers Abroad

We do not sell personal data. We disclose personal data only to the service providers named above (Formspree, Google, GitHub), to professional advisers where necessary, or where required by law. Some of these providers are located in the United States; transfers are safeguarded by the Swiss–U.S. Data Privacy Framework and/or standard contractual clauses.

5. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by statutory retention obligations. Enquiry correspondence is deleted when it is no longer needed for the business relationship.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about the personal data we process about you, to request rectification or deletion, to object to processing, and to request restriction of processing. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time with effect for the future. If the GDPR applies to you, you also have the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The competent Swiss authority is the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).

To exercise your rights, contact us using the address above or via our contact page.

7. Cookies

This website uses cookies only for the web analytics described above. You can block or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings; the website remains fully functional without cookies.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The version published on this page is the current version.
