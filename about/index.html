About Global Key Partners

A boutique access and introduction firm connecting private capital with curated off-market opportunities. built on trust, discretion, and genuine relationships.

Our mission

Access Is the True Scarcity

Global Key Partners was founded on a simple but profound insight: the most valuable opportunities in private markets are never publicly listed. They exist at the intersection of trust, timing, and genuine relationships, accessible only to those with the right presence in the right places.

GKP was built to be that presence. We operate as a discreet field partner for investors, principals, and asset owners who need a trusted, permanently active intermediary in the Swiss and global private markets. We listen. We filter. We connect.

The company's value lies not in volume, but in access, discretion, timing, and trust.

The founding team

The Founders

GKP's competitive advantage is rooted in the personal credibility and proprietary networks of its founders.

Vlora Rexhepi

Co-Founder & Managing Partner

Vlora Rexhepi is the Founder and Managing Partner of Global Key Partners, leading the firm's relationships, partnerships, and mandates across Switzerland, Europe, and Southeast Asia. She holds an MBA from the Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts and brings more than a decade of senior leadership experience across the luxury, hospitality, education, and private members' club sectors. Having led international teams and projects across the DACH region and Hong Kong, she has developed extensive relationships with family offices, private investors, entrepreneurs, luxury brands, hospitality groups, and international business communities. As a shareholder in a high-end hospitality venue in Lucerne, Vlora combines strategic business development expertise with a deep understanding of relationship-driven industries, forming the foundation of GKP's approach to connecting people, capital, and opportunities.

What we stand for

Our Principles

I

Trust over transaction

Every relationship GKP builds is intended to last. We never sacrifice relationship quality for transaction volume.

II

Discretion as default

GKP does not disclose client names, transaction details, or counterparty information without explicit consent.

III

Quality over quantity

GKP works with a limited number of mandates at any time, ensuring each receives full focus and commitment.

IV

Alignment of interest

GKP participates in equity and revenue where appropriate, ensuring its interests are genuinely aligned with those of its clients.

Working with GKP

Global Key Partners works with a select group of investors, entrepreneurs, family offices, and asset owners. Where there is a potential alignment of interests and objectives, GKP welcomes an initial confidential conversation.
